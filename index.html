Branding Ideas

The company "One Cake Boutique" is a cake/dessert brand, aiming to provide both great taste and amazing visual experiences for the customers. The company would not only make classic styles of cakes but also would like to tie the popular elements into the products. The concept and slogan of the company would be "Stay Simple, Stay True."



The potential customers would mainly be young groups according to the concept and design of the brand, but also could be cake lovers for all age groups.



With the enthusiasm of beautiful cake, One Cake Boutique would challenge the status-quo of cake making and cake design, preferring to integrate classic styles and the latest popular elements, to mostly satisfy the demands of cusomers. The delicate designs for the wrap and other related products, as well as the brand concept will also be a good attraction for the customers, which matches the current popular elements.
